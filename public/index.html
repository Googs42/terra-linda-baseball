GameChanger + MaxPreps Sync
Automatic stat syncing - score once, it appears everywhere
Once set up, every game you score in GameChanger automatically pushes batting stats, pitching stats, and scores directly to your MaxPreps page. No double entry.
1
Set up roster & schedule on MaxPreps first
Log into Coach Admin and make sure your players and games are entered before moving to GameChanger
2
Create your team in GameChanger
Open the GameChanger app, tap +, choose School team type, select High School, then select Varsity or JV
3
Type in Terra Linda and select the school
GameChanger will find your MaxPreps page and offer to import your schedule and roster automatically
4
Enable stat sync
In GameChanger settings, tap the gear icon, go to Stat Settings, and turn on "Yes, sync stats with MaxPreps"
5
Score games in GameChanger as normal
After each game, stats upload to GameChanger and push automatically to MaxPreps. Done.
Why MaxPreps matters for your players
College scouts search MaxPreps
Recruiters at every level use MaxPreps to find players. An updated profile gets your guys seen.
State rankings & recognition
The more games you score, the higher your ranking confidence and visibility statewide.
Parents get live notifications
Any parent who follows TL on MaxPreps gets score alerts, schedule reminders, and stat updates.
Career stats follow your players
Stats from every season stack up on each player's profile automatically over their high school career.

GameChanger Auto-Sync
Score a game once - stats go everywhere automatically
How it works
Enter roster and schedule on MaxPreps first. Connect GameChanger to MaxPreps. Score games live on GameChanger. Stats auto-sync to MaxPreps - parents see everything in real time.
1
Set up your MaxPreps coach account
Go to maxpreps.com - sign in or create a free coach admin account for Terra Linda Trojans Baseball
Create account at maxpreps.com/signup
2
Enter roster and schedule on MaxPreps
Use the Export CSV button in your Roster section to download your roster, then upload it directly into your MaxPreps coach admin. Add your game schedule manually or via CSV.
3
Download GameChanger and create your team
Free app for iOS and Android. When creating your team choose: School > High School > Varsity. Type Terra Linda and select it from the dropdown.
Download GameChanger at gc.com
4
Select "Import MaxPreps Roster" when prompted
GameChanger will pull your roster and schedule directly from MaxPreps. This only works at initial team setup - do not skip this step.
5
Enable "Sync stats with MaxPreps" in GameChanger settings
In the GameChanger app go to your team > gear icon > Stat Settings > turn on MaxPreps sync. From now on every game you score auto-posts to MaxPreps.

About our outreach program

Terra Linda Baseball players and coaches are available to visit elementary schools, little league organizations, and youth programs throughout Marin County.

Our varsity players serve as mentors and instructors - teaching fundamental skills, sportsmanship, and a love of the game to younger players.

We offer free 1-hour introductory visits and paid half-day or full-day clinic packages. Fill out the contact form and we will be in touch within 48 hours.

What we offer
School visit (1 hour, free)
2-3 varsity players, fundamentals demo & Q&A
Little league practice assist (2 hours, $150)
Coach + 4 players, run stations with your team
Half-day skills clinic ($350)
Full clinic with hitting, fielding, and pitching
Full-day tournament prep camp ($600)
Game situations, team defense, live BP
